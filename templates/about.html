What Is The OCC About?

Hello, thanks for visiting this site! In these uncertain times
many people are confused, worried, and even scared with all the sudden changes
occurring in our lives as the coronavirus disease has been spreading.

With the world in lockdown, many people (like you!) have gained a lot of free time,
which (and I can personally verify this) goes to waste because we don't know how to
spend it. In addition to this, new discoveries about Covid-19 are made everyday, and
it can be hard to keep up with the latest scientific research, what it means, and how
this may affect us.

These two principles are what will guide this site and its content. You can access a
page where I (as a student of biology) compile the latest scientific updates on Covid-19,
explain their basis, and how this might affect you (in terms of your lifestyle).
Additionally, I'll be creating and updating pages on different activities that you can
keep yourself busy with as we all stay at home; almost like mini-blogs! It doesn't end there
though, as I have a lot of further plans for this website! Again, thanks for visiting, and I
hope you enjoy yourself!
